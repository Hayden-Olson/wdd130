The world is ending in a month, and everyone is in a panic!

Play as Rocket, a young boy undeterred by the chaos, and battle against foes who wish to take advantage of the havoc. Clash against the military, who race against you for the hidden magic gems to use for their own agenda. Utilize the environment to your advantage and make your way to the Chaos Caster, before the world reaches a fate unknown to all.

Manage your team effectively! Keep up their spirits and strategize how to effectively take out your foes. Customize your team and decide how you want to fight! Drive around and explore the world! Speed by with the rocket punch and use other partner abilities for mobility in your exploration! Fly around and gun down other enemy ships! Test your reflexes in chase sequences! If you are feeling daring, race against the clock and save the world before time runs out in Hard Mode! All of this and more in Mech Quest!
